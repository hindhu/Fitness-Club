Yoga

Yoga Basics

Whether you are a beginner or feel the need to get back to basics, our teachers will guide and support you through this class one pose at a time leading to improved strength, flexibility and body awareness.

Yoga Vinyasa

Using a flowing series of postures to create heat within your body, you will increase strength, flexibility, endurance and balance. Usually set to upbeat music, the teacher first guides the class through the flow together. Then, each student has the freedom to move at his or her own pace.Yoga Vinyasa is for anyone wanting to dive into a multi-level, strong practice.
